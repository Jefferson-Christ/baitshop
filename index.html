Welcome to the Lakeside

Welcome to Howard's Lakeside General Store! Nestled in the heart of our Ohio Springfield community, we offer a wide variety of goods to meet all your fishing needs. Whether you're gearing up for a day on the water, picking up essentials, or searching for a special gift. From outdoor gear and fishing supplies to delicious treats, our store is a one-stop shop for both locals and visitors alike. Stop by, say hello, and let us help make your lakeside experience even better!
